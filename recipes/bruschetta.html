Bruschetta

Freshly made Bruschetta

The recipe is easy to follow and it only takes 30min to do

For 2 portions

Ingedients

  • 4 tomatoes
  • 3 garlic gloves
  • 75ml of olive oil
  • handful basil leafs
  • tea spoon of salt and pepper
  • ciabatta bread

Steps

  1. cut tomatoes in small dices
  2. mince the garlic gloves
  3. mince the basil leaves
  4. put them in all together in a bowl and add the olive oil and salt and pepper
  5. mix thoroughly
  6. let it sit for 1hour
  7. bake the bread until crispy and golden brown
  8. put the tomato mixture on top of the bread
